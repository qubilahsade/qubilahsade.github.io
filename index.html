Communion© is an online resource created to facilitate the sharing exchange of intimate thoughts and feelings between black women -- especially those who find themselves on the front line of fighting social & racial injustice. While we must unapologetically love ourselves, loving within a community sustains us.

Inspired after reading bell hooks' "Communion: The Female Search for Love", the founder launched Communion to help affirm and uplift women who have & continue to endure roads less traveled.

It is the hope that through this space, women from across the globe, -- whether corporate or self-employed, spiritual or not-so-spiritual, free-spirited or a caged-bird -- will find solidarity, love, & sisterhood.
